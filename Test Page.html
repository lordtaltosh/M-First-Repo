How to make a sandwhich

  1. Place one piece of bread down
  2. Coat one side of bread with condiment of choice
  3. Add meat of choice
  4. Add any other topping you like
  5. Add cheese of choice
  6. Add another condiment if you like
  7. Finish with another slice of bread
